A (brief) introduction

I'll describe who I am currently — minus nitty-gritty career and academic aspects because there's plenty of that in the about page.

I'm Fides, and I have always been drawn to visual arts. It's probably the one thing that's been constant in my life, and I try to include it in everything I do. I started with traditional mediums first (pencil and paper) and then transitioned into digital illustration and graphic design.

I like to make everything look nice and presentable — be it my clothes (I also love ethical fashion), my writing, and of course — my development work. It's a big reason why I do web development. I find that there's plenty of ways to get creative with it.

When I'm not doing "programming-related" stuff, I'm usually shopping. I also volunteer as a designer for student-run organizations. It's a way for me to contribute doing what I'm good at, and to improve at this hobby.

If you meet me in real life, the first thing you'd probably notice is that I'm quite reserved. I am small in appearance, and I don't talk much. I don't think I'm scary though (in contrast — I'm usually intimated by other people.) So if you see me in real life, you can say hi!
