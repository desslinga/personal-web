Concept art for U of T Hacks VI, created as a request. This design was not eventually used for the event, but it was still fun making it!
The U of T Hacks brand has consistently revolved around the Toronto skyline at night, and I decided to continue that.

The CN Tower was the scariest part to draw, as it would be the highlight. It turned out well though!
Skyline buildings took up the majority of the piece. For this, the atmosphere I was aiming for was calm, peaceful, and warm.

Thus, I gave the building lights, as well as the tiered skylines a soft glow as they fade into the distance.
Lastly, the title. To make it just a bit more magical, I added sparks around it, complementing the warm glow of the skyline.
