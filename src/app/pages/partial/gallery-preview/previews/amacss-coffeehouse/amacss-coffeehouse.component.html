These are marketing assets I created for the Coffee House event at AMACSS. It is a casual networking event for professors and students in the Computer Science / Mathematics / Statistics departments. I was tasked with creating a Facebook banner, print poster, and an invitation card to be distributed to guest professors.
The previous year's design revolved around coffee and dessert. Although refreshments were part of the event, I thought that the "mix and mingle" aspect was not being portrayed well enough. So, wanted to change things up!

The new theme mixed networking with the previous theme of refreshments. The goal of this design was to create a homely and inviting look, as networking events can be intimidating sometimes!

The illustration is of professors/students who are attending Coffee House. I took some inspiration from various illustration styles, and characters I saw on the Behance website.
The handwritten text and border style is meant to emulate a paper invitation, all complemented by a palette full of warm tones (with blue to remain true to the AMACSS brand). As for the actual characters themselves... they are obviously fictional, but inspired by actual professors at UTSC!
Some final thoughts...

I had lots of fun making this project! It was quite the learning experience too. Normally, I would stay clear of drawing people for any of my designs, but I thought it would be way more interesting if I did, specially for this project! To (comfortably) get myself out of my comfort zone, I did use reference pictures.

Overall, I'm happy with how these pieces worked out. Thanks so much for reading!
