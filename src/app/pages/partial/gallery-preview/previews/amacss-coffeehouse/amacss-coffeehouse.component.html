These are marketing assets I created for the Coffee House event at AMACSS. It is a casual networking event for professors and students in the Computer Science / Mathematics / Statistics departments.
The theme is coffee and dessert, plus networking. The goal of the design was to create a homely and inviting look, as networking events can be intimidating sometimes!

The illustration is of professors/students who are attending Coffee House.
The handwritten text and border style is meant to emulate a paper invitation, all complemented by a palette full of warm tones (with blue to remain true to the AMACSS brand).
