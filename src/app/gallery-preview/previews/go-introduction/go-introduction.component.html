During my internship with GO Transit, one of my projects was to design a brochure that was to be given to customers who were new to GO.
I designed the cover, and inner pages with custom vector illustrations using GO's design guidelines.

Since the goal of the brochure was an introduction, I went for a playful, simple theme. We wanted it to be easy to read and digest.
I used Adobe Illustrator to create vectors for select Toronto transit vehicles.
